Privacy Policy for Marginalia

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Marginalia. This privacy policy explains how Marginalia handles your information.

Marginalia is designed with your privacy as a top priority. The App operates with a "privacy-first" approach, meaning we collect and store as little data as possible.

2. What Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Your writing: All drafts, notes, and manuscripts you create are stored locally on your device.
  • Chapter cards: Any outline or card information is stored locally.
  • App settings: Your theme preferences, daily word count goals, and settings are stored locally.

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • No personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • No usage analytics
  • No crash reports
  • No IP addresses
  • No device information
  • No location data
  • No cookies

3. How Your Data is Stored

Local Storage Only: All your data is stored exclusively on your own device using your browser's localStorage. Nothing is ever sent to our servers.

Backup Options:

  • Export/Import: You can manually export your project as a JSON file.
  • Google Drive (Optional): If you choose to use this, your data is stored in YOUR Google Drive account.
  • Email (Optional): If you choose to send your manuscript via email, it's sent through YOUR Google account.

4. Google Services (Optional Features)

Google Drive Backup:

  • The App requests permission to read/write files to YOUR Google Drive
  • We only access files that contain "marginalia_backup_" in the filename
  • We never access any other files in your Drive

Gmail Integration:

  • The App requests permission to send emails through YOUR Gmail account
  • We only send the email you compose within the App
  • We never read your emails or access your contacts

Google Sign-In:

  • The App uses Google Sign-In solely for authentication
  • We receive your name and email address ONLY to display it in the App
  • We never store or share this information

5. Third-Party Services

Marginalia may include links to third-party websites. These sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

6. Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • We cannot access your data
  • We cannot lose your data
  • You are responsible for backing up your own data

For optional Google services:

  • Your data is protected by Google's security measures
  • The App uses OAuth 2.0, which means we never see your password
  • You can revoke the App's access at any time through your Google Account settings

7. Children's Privacy

Marginalia is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date.

9. Your Rights

  • Right to access: Your data is on your device - you can see it anytime
  • Right to delete: Clear your browser's localStorage
  • Right to export: Use the "Export Project" feature
  • Right to withdraw consent: Revoke access in your Google Account settings
