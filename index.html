Marginalia - FAQ

Purpose: A distraction-free drafting environment for writers.

Features

Spell Check

Words are checked against a built-in dictionary. Misspelled words appear with a red wavy underline. Right-click or click on underlined words to see suggested corrections.

Email Send

Send your manuscript via email using your Gmail account directly through the app. Click "Sign in with Google" to connect.

Google Drive Backup

Backup and restore your project using Google Drive. Click "Sign in with Google" to connect.

PWA Installation

Click "Install Now" to add Marginalia to your home screen. Works on mobile and desktop browsers.

Search & Replace

Find text in your document and optionally replace it with new text.

Custom Dictionary

Add custom words to your dictionary so they aren't flagged as misspelled.

Writing Timer

A Pomodoro-style timer to track your writing sessions.

Writing Streak

Track your daily writing streaks (100 words minimum per day).

Export Options

Export as Markdown, HTML, TXT, Word (DOCX), or EPUB.

Marginalia is free and always will be.
