Marginalia - FAQ

Purpose: A distraction-free drafting environment for writers who draft from existing outlines and chapter cards.

Features

Import

Import manuscript files (TXT, MD, DOCX) or chapter cards (TXT, MD).

Export

Export your manuscript to Email, Google Drive, or Local storage in TXT, MD, or DOCX formats.

Search & Replace

Press Ctrl+F (Cmd+F on Mac) to find and replace text in your manuscript.

Themes

Switch between Dark Draft, Paper Draft, Manuscript, and E-Ink modes.

Google Drive & Email

Sign in with Google to enable email sending and Drive backup features.

Marginalia is free and always will be.
