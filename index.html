Marginalia - FAQ

Purpose: A distraction-free drafting environment for writers who draft from existing outlines and chapter cards.

Features

Import

Import manuscript files (TXT, MD) or chapter cards (TXT, MD).

Export

Export your manuscript to Email (TXT, MD) or Google Drive (TXT, MD), or Local (TXT, MD).

Themes

Switch between Dark Draft, Paper Draft, Manuscript, E-Ink, and Pride Rainbow modes.

Google Drive & Email

Sign in with Google to enable email sending and Drive backup features.
